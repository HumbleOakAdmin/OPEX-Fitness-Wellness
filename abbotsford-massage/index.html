Karilee Ennis

Registered Massage Therapist

Book Appointment

About Karilee

Karilee graduated from the West Coast College of Massage Therapy and has been a practicing RMT since 2020. During her practicum she gained a wealth of knowledge and experience treating different orthopedic and systemic conditions.

She had the opportunity to work with high-risk pregnancies and labours at BC Womens and Royal Columbian Hospital. Her interest in the human body and passion to help people lead to her career in health care. She welcomes clients of all stages and conditions during their journey to wellness.

She uses a combination of techniques such as, but not limited to:  myofascial mobilization, cranial sacral, trigger point release, passive and active stretching to address her client's individual needs.

Her desire to continue learning has lead her to take continuing courses in fascial mobilization, pre/post natal care, cranial sacral, and TMJ dysfunction.

Starting in the Spring of 2024 she will be furthering her knowledge as she starts the Biodynamic Fascial Integrated series. Which is focused in the areas of Craniosacral Therapy, Visceral Manipulation and Fascial Release.

During her spare time she enjoys practicing yoga and spending time outside with her husband, two young boys and dog!

About Kaed

Kaed is a Registered Massage Therapist who completed his training at the Vancouver College of Massage Therapy in 2024. He enjoys working with a variety of different conditions, injuries, or with patients who come simply to relax. Kaed uses a variety of different techniques such as deep tissue, stretching, myofascial work, and relaxation techniques depending on the individual and their needs. Whether you are dealing with a recent injury or are dealing with chronic pain from a condition or past injury, Kaed welcomes patients of all stages of wellness during their journey towards improving function and meeting their treatment goals. Outside of massage therapy, Kaed also works as a line cook part time and loves to rock climb.

Abbotsford Massage Therapy

Massage therapy is a type of treatment in which a trained and registered professional (RMT) manipulates the soft tissue of your body (joints, muscles, ligaments, tendons, connective tissue and skin, using a variety of techniques, pressure and movement, with the goals to alleviate pain, improve blood flow and mobility, and promote healing.

Registered Massage Therapists (RMTs) in British Columbia are health care professionals committed to restoring and maintaining optimal health for their patients. They are educated to assess and treat using a variety of techniques.
