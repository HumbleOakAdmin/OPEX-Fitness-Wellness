How Does our Small Group Personal Training Work?

Consider this to be our “hybrid” model of personal training. Basically, you get all of the perks of having a personal trainer, at a cheaper price point. With this training service plan, you will receive a:

‍Dedicated Personal Coach who performs your assessment, creates your training program, helps you with your nutrition and lifestyle and hold you accountable so you can get attain your goals!

‍Personalized Training Program for you to follow based on your goals and abilities. This program progresses as you progress. No random workouts, no cookie cutter templates.

‍Monthly Consultations with your personal coach to discuss your training plan and provide nutrition and lifestyle guidance.

‍Unlimited Private Communication with your personal coach through our app in case plans change and or you have a question.

‍Flexible Training Schedule because we know life can be hectic sometimes. Your coach plans your training week out for you, but ultimately you decide when you workout based on the day/times that work for you. Each time you come into OPEX, there will be a coach on the floor to help facilitate your workout.

‍Private Gym with Floor Coach who will float around the room checking in on you and your peers to make sure you are performing your training plan safely, answer any questions, and be there to support you.

As you can see, the main difference between this plan and 1-on-1 personal training is that your personal coach is not necessarily with you at every workout to watch you exercise. If you are looking for that type of personal training, we also offer it and would be happy to work with you! Instead, you’ll be working out in our private gym with one of our floor coaches who will support you and make sure you are safely following your training plan that your personal coach designed for you. We’ll ensure to match you up with a coach that will be floor coaching during the times you’ll likely be in the gym. This way you get to work directly with your own coach.

This training plan is what makes OPEX Abbotsford unique from other personal trainers. Book a free 30-minute consultation with us today and we’d be happy to meet with you, tell you more about this program and see if this is a fit!
