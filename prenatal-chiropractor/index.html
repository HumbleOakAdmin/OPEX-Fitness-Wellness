What is Prenatal Chiropractic Care?

Prenatal chiropractic care is a specialized branch of chiropractic care that focuses on the unique needs of pregnant women. Chiropractic care during pregnancy involves gentle adjustments and techniques tailored to the changing body of the expectant mother.

Chiropractors who specialize in prenatal care receive additional training to understand the physiological and biomechanical changes that occur during pregnancy.

Pregnancy leads to various physical and hormonal changes in a woman's body, including an increased curvature of the back, changes in the pelvis, and a shift in the center of gravity.

These changes can result in misalignments in the spine or joints, causing discomfort and pain. Prenatal chiropractic care aims to address these issues and promote overall well-being for both the mother and the baby.
