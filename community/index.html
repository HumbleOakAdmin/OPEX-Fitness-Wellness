Join us for the launch of our brand new location in Chilliwack BC

We’re excited to share that OPEX is expanding with a second location opening soon in Chilliwack!

This growth is a direct reflection of the incredible community that has supported us in Abbotsford from day one. Our new Chilliwack facility will bring the same personalized coaching, welcoming atmosphere, and high-quality wellness services you’ve come to expect—now with even more room for our community to grow.

We can’t wait to welcome new members, strengthen our existing connections, and continue helping people achieve their health and fitness goals together.
