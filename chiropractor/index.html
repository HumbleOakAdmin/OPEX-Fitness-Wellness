About Yafa

Dr. Elseri commenced her path to becoming a chiropractor by graduating from the University of Alberta in 2006 with a Bachelors of Science degree.  Following graduation she moved to San Jose, California where she obtained her Doctor of Chiropractic degree (Cum Laude) from Palmer College of Chiropractic West in 2009. She is a member of the Phi Delta Chiropractic Honor Society for outstanding academic achievement.

Dr. Elseri has practiced in Alberta for 5 years and has been practicing in British Columbia since 2017.  She is keenly focused on each individual patient and strives to provide personalized care based off each patient's needs and goals.  She is certified in Active Release Techniques and is a Graston Technique provider.  In addition to traditional chiropractic adjustments, Dr. Elseri treats soft tissue as an integral part of proper spinal biomechanics. She provides safe, gentle and effective care for a wide range of patients from the small child with postural concerns, to the elite athlete, to her oldest patient who is 91 years old. She recognizes that we are all unique and what works for one person may not work for the next.

Outside of practicing Dr. Elseri has always had a passion for athletics.  She played competitive ringette for 14 years and was selected to play for the US National Ringette Team at the World Championships.  Currently Dr. Elseri still makes fitness a priority while enjoying motherhood with her daughter and son.

What can you expect at an OPEX Chiro appointment?

If this is your first time booking with OPEX Abbotsford Chiropractic you’ll want to book our Initial Assessment. This appointment takes up to 60 minutes and is designed for first-time patients or if you haven’t seen one of our Abbotsford chiropractors in over a year. The goal is to understand your chiropractic needs, medical history, and perform treatment in this appointment.

You’ll be greeted by your Chiropractor, and they will begin by asking about what type of pain or discomfort you are experiencing that requires chiropractic care. They will ask a few questions about your medical history and injury history, before conducting a brief physical assessment.

Your chiropractor will then perform chiropractic treatment on you to help alleviate the pain or discomfort that brought you to the appointment. Once treatment is complete, you’ll talk and determine the best next steps and design a holistic treatment plan tailored to your needs.

Chiropractic Treatment Techniques Offered

Your OPEX chiropractor is a highly trained practitioner and may use one or multiple of these treatment techniques in your appointment to help alleviate pain or discomfort.

Diversified Technique

Restores spinal alignment, addresses joint dysfunction, and ensures optimal movement and mobility. Through precise hands-on thrusts, practitioners can realign the spine and enhance a patient's range of motion.

Spinal Mobilization

Alleviates joint pressure, reduces inflammation, and enhances nerve function. Similar to the diversified technique, spinal mobilization involves gentler thrusting motions and incorporates more stretching techniques.

Drop-Table Technique

This allows the practitioner to administer a quick thrust while simultaneously lowering the patient a fraction of an inch. The gentle dropping motion resembles a light vibration and ensures comfort during the adjustment.

Webster Technique (Prenatal Chiropractic)

Gentle adjustments and analysis used during pregnancy to address pelvic imbalances and optimize pelvic alignment. It aims to improve comfort for the mother and create a more favorable environment for the baby.

Graston Technique

Involves the use of specialized instruments to detect and treat soft tissue restrictions through controlled strokes and pressure. The goal is to break down areas of scar tissue, adhesions, and fascial restrictions.

Cupping

Alleviates muscle tension, promotes relaxation, and stimulates the body's natural healing process. The skin and tissues are drawn upwards into the cup, resulting in increased blood flow and improved circulation.

Active Release Technique

Identifies and treats soft tissue, scar tissue, and muscle imbalances that can cause pain, restricted range of motion, and reduced performance. The treatment is a mix of precise movements and targeted tension.

Bioflex Laser Therapy

Uses specific wavelengths of light to target damaged tissue and promote healing. Reduces pain and inflammation, speeds up healing, and improves range of motion. This treatment is safe and non-invasive.

Chiropractor Services

Chiropractic is a non-invasive, hands-on health care discipline that focuses on the neuro-musculo-skeletal system. The main form of treatment chiropractors deliver is called an adjustment - which is a precise directed movement to help relieve pain and discomfort, and restore range of motion. Our chiropractors also utilize Graston, Active Release Technique and other soft tissue techniques in their practice.

After taking a complete history and diagnosing a patient, a chiropractor can develop and carry out a comprehensive treatment/management plan, recommend therapeutic exercise and other non-invasive therapies, and provide nutritional, dietary and lifestyle counselling.
