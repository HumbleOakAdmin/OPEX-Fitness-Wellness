How OPEX Works

Uniquely Local.

Train Anywhere. At the gym or at home.

STEP ONE

Initial Consultation

In order to create a program that best suits you, your coach will spend time getting to know who you are, what motivates you in fitness, and why you have set certain goals.
STEP TWO

Physical Assesment

The physical assessment works in 3 parts:
‍BODY: We do a full body composition analysis.
‍MOVE: We look at how you move and identify any limitations or imbalances in your movement.
‍WORK: We do an initial fitness test to see where you stand in your fitness journey.
STEP THREE

Let's get to work!

Based on your goals, lifestyle, and physical assessment, your professional coach will create a long-term plan that will help you accomplish your goals and much more. When you are in the gym there will always be a coach there to support you and answer questions. If you are training remotely, no sweat! We use state of the art software to deliver training and communicate with you.
